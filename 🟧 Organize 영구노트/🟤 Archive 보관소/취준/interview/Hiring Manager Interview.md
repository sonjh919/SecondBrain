## 1. 리더십 & 팀 협업 관련 (Bar Raiser 핵심 영역)

**성장 잠재력 (Growth Potential) 평가:**

- "Tell me about a time when you had to learn a completely new technology or domain to solve a problem. How did you approach it?"
    
- "Describe a situation where you received critical feedback. How did you respond and what did you learn?"
    
- "Where do you see yourself growing in the next 2-3 years? What skills are you actively developing?"
    

**실용적 문제 해결 (Pragmatic Problem-Solving):**

- "Walk me through a complex technical problem you solved recently. What trade-offs did you consider?"
    
- "Tell me about a time when you had to make a technical decision with incomplete information. How did you proceed?"
    
- "Describe a situation where you had to balance technical excellence with business urgency. How did you decide?"
    

**협업 & 장기적 임팩트 (Collaboration & Long-term Impact):**

- "Tell me about a time when you disagreed with a team member or manager. How did you handle it?"
    
- "Describe how you've mentored or helped grow junior engineers on your team."
    
- "Give me an example of a project where you had to work cross-functionally. What challenges did you face?"
    
- "How do you ensure your technical decisions create long-term value for the team and product?"
    

## 2. 기술적 깊이 & 프로젝트 경험

**당신의 주요 경험 관련:**

- "Tell me about your work on the read-model search features with 800k+ datasets. What were the key performance bottlenecks and how did you optimize them?"
    
- "Explain your approach to database indexing and query optimization. Can you give me a specific example?"
    
- "Describe your experience with MySQL full-text search implementation. What challenges did you encounter?"
    
- "Walk me through your infrastructure setup process. How do you ensure high availability and scalability?"
    

**Backend Architecture & System Design:**

- "How would you design a high-throughput backend system that needs to handle millions of requests per day?"
    
- "Explain your approach to connection pooling and resource management in production systems."
    
- "Tell me about your experience with monitoring and observability. What tools and practices do you use?"
    
- "How do you approach load testing and performance benchmarking for a new feature?"
    

## 3. Delivery Hero 특화 질문

**회사 & 제품 이해도:**

- "Why do you want to join Delivery Hero specifically? What excites you about our mission?"
    
- "What do you know about Delivery Hero's tech stack and engineering culture?"
    
- "How would you contribute to improving our platform's reliability and performance?"
    
- "What challenges do you think a global delivery platform like Delivery Hero faces, and how would you address them?"
    

**팀 문화 & 가치관:**

- "Describe your ideal team environment. What does effective collaboration look like to you?"
    
- "How do you handle working with distributed teams across different time zones?"
    
- "Tell me about a time when you had to adapt to a significant change in your team or company. How did you handle it?"
    

## 4. 행동 기반 질문 (Behavioral - Bar Raiser 스타일)

**실패와 학습:**

- "Tell me about your biggest technical failure. What did you learn and how did you apply that learning?"
    
- "Describe a project that didn't go as planned. What would you do differently?"
    

**우선순위 & 의사결정:**

- "How do you prioritize between technical debt and new feature development?"
    
- "Tell me about a time when you had to say 'no' to a stakeholder. How did you communicate it?"
    
- "Describe a situation where you had to make a difficult trade-off decision under pressure."
    

**주니어 개발자와의 협업:**

- "How do you deal with junior engineers in your team? Can you give me a specific example?"
    
- "Tell me about a time when you had to give constructive feedback to a teammate."
    

## 5. 기술적 심층 질문 (Backend Engineer 관련)

**Java/Spring Boot 전문성:**

- "Explain your experience with Spring Boot in production. What performance optimizations have you implemented?"
    
- "How do you handle thread management and concurrent programming in Java applications?"
    
- "Tell me about your approach to REST API design. What principles do you follow?"
    

**Database & Performance:**

- "Explain how you would troubleshoot a slow query affecting production. Walk me through your process."
    
- "What's your experience with database fragmentation? How do you monitor and maintain it?"
    
- "How do you approach caching strategies in a high-traffic backend system?"
    

**Infrastructure & DevOps:**

- "Describe your experience with AWS. What services have you used and how?"
    
- "How do you approach CI/CD pipeline setup for backend services?"
    
- "What's your strategy for monitoring and alerting in production systems?"
